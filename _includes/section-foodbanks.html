The House of Bread's Food Bank...

The House of Bread Food Bank has two main functions. The first function is to supplement those who have food but for some reason need that extra support. This could be someone who has to decide between paying bills or buying food, or an elderly person who may not be able to leave the house to buy food due to ill health or bad weather. The second function is to supply people who are either homeless or without regular accommodation.

How it Works...

  1. You donate food at our various collection points (Asda, Tesco, Stafford Railway Station)

  2. We collect, organise and store the materials

  3. The materials are then distributed to various Foodbank organisations who find them a good home! :)
